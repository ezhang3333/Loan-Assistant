## Project Summary

We are going to build an app that helps users navigate the confusing and messy loan approval process. We want to provide users with an initial pre-qualification score and bank matching display that will take personal inputs from the user and estimate an approval probability based on a historical loan dataset. Our app will then rank lenders by a fit score that would include factors such as probability of approval, distance from their current location, loan type, etc. Users will then be able to select whichever bank they are interested in applying for, and our app will list a set of the biggest risk factors. This step in the loan approval process will also provide some actionable steps that users should or could take in order to increase their chances of approval. We also want the user to be able to calculate how their probability would change based on some actionable changes they might make.
	This application should apply to anyone considering or wondering about taking out a loan, and the types of loans we are currently aiming for are: home, auto, and personal loans.
